What We Offer

Sydney's Trusted Painting Experts

At North Shore Painting, we deliver beautiful, long-lasting paint finishes that transform your home or commercial space. Whether you need a full interior repaint, exterior refresh, or a striking feature wall, our experienced painters ensure a flawless result every time.

We use only premium paints from trusted brands like Dulux and Taubmans, combined with proper surface preparation to ensure your paint job looks stunning and lasts for years.

  • Interior Painting Complete interior transformations including walls, ceilings, trim, and doors with a flawless, even finish.
  • Exterior Painting Weather-resistant exterior coatings that protect and beautify your home's facade, eaves, and trim.
  • Feature Walls Bold accent walls and decorative finishes that add character and personality to any room.
  • Cabinet Repainting Refresh tired kitchen and bathroom cabinets with durable, factory-quality spray finishes.
  • Commercial Painting Professional painting for offices, retail spaces, and strata common areas with minimal disruption.
  • Colour Consulting Expert colour advice to help you choose the perfect palette for your space, style, and lighting.

How Your Painting Project Works

From first contact to final coat, here's what to expect.

01

Painting FAQ

A standard 3-bedroom home typically takes 3–5 days for a full interior repaint. This includes preparation (filling holes, sanding, masking), priming where needed, and applying two coats of premium paint. Larger homes or those requiring extensive prep work may take longer. We'll provide an exact timeline in your quote.
